Why I Did Not Finish High School
Going through high school, I found several of my peers unmotivated, disinterested, or simply unfocused. This starkly contrasted with my personal motivation to find my passions and pursue my future as a productive member of society. This led me to look into non-traditional educational opportunities, which eventually led me to pursue a GED and "drop out" of high school after learning about my current institution, Bard College at Simon's Rock.

What is Bard College at Simon's Rock?
Bard College at Simon's Rock is America's only 4-year early college program, accepting students who have completed the 10th grade. Here are some facts about Simon's Rock:

  • 95% of the full-time faculty at Simon's Rock hold the highest degree in their field
  • Simon's Rock has produced 9 Fulbright Fellows since 2010
  • Simon's Rock has a student faculty ratio of 8:1
  • Simon's Rock received an academic rating of 99 from the Princeton Review - a score that is equal to Yale, but higher than both Harvard and even Princeton
