About

Introduction
Hello, my name is Vijay Pillai. I am a college junior at Bard College at Simon's Rock and am majoring in computer science. If you are visiting this website, it most likely means that I have applied for a job or internship at your workplace. After you have read my resume, however, you will discover that my educational background is a rather odd one that will bring about several questions including: "Why haven't you finished high school?" and "What and where is Bard College at Simon's Rock?" It is, sadly, somewhat likely that these questions will cause potential employers to doubt in my abilities as an individual, student, and computer scientist. My goal in writing this page, however, is to dispel these notions.

Why I Did Not Finish High School
Going through high school, I found several of my peers unmotivated, disinterested, or simply unfocused. This starkly contrasted with my personal motivation to find my passions and pursue my future as a productive member of society. This led me to look into non-traditional educational opportunities, which eventually led me to pursue a GED and "drop out" of high school after learning about my current institution, Bard College at Simon's Rock.

What is Bard College at Simon's Rock?
Bard College at Simon's Rock is America's only 4-year early college program, accepting students who have completed the 10th grade. Now, one might hear the words "early college" and immediately begin to doubt the quality of education that I am receiving. I urge you, however, to consider otherwise:

  • 95% of the full-time faculty at Simon's Rock hold the highest degree in their field.
  • Simon's Rock has produced 9 Fulbright Fellows since 2010.
  • Simon's Rock has a student faculty ratio of 8:1
  • Simon's Rock received an academic rating of 99 from the Princeton Review - a score that is equal to Yale, but higher than both Harvard and even Princeton

Conclusion
Simon's Rock has been and is providing me with a one-of-a-kind education. While it does not exactly ring true with "traditional," it is exceptional. I may be younger and possibly less experienced than your other applicants, but you will not find a more dedicated or motivated applicant for your workplace.
